Introduction


The Digital Wallet Muslim Youth Welfare Kit


A digital Wallet is an electronic device,
virtual wallet or online service that allows one to make electronic transactions,
to store and manage currency. In this concept, the digital wallet is to be introduced to the youth particularly highschool and university students
It will help curb the many problems and issues faced by such a group.
As from the data and information obtained from a certain research,
it is seen that many youths face financial problems and many other problems in that introducing such a program to the group,
it may be of better help to them and even to the society. Therefore, the target group for such a program is the youth in our society( Highschool and University stundents).


Our Mission


To provide a secure, user-friendly and educational platform.
This will empowers the youth( highschool and university students)
to manage their finances, save, invest and make digital payments while promoting financial literacy
and responsible spending habits. To empower small businesses run by the youths
by providing them with a comprehensive set of tools and resources to create finances.


Our vision


To foster innovations and convenience in digital transactions.
