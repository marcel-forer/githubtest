Our Business Model

Transaction Fees

The digital wallet welfare kit will charge a small fee
for each transaction conducted through their platform.
This could be a percentage of the transaction amount or a fixed fee.

Partnerships With Financial Institutions

The digital wallet kit may partner with banks and financial institutions
to offer services like savings accounts, loans and investment options.
The wallet provider will therefore earn a commission or fee for facilitating these services.

Subscription Plans

The digital wallet may offer premium/subscription plans
with added features such as enhanced security, additional rewards,
higher transaction limits and more. Users therefore may pay a monthly
or annual fee for these plans hence generating consistent income for the digital wallet welfare kit.
