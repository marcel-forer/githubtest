Registration

To use a digital wallet, a user needs
to sign up and create an account.
This involves providing personal information
and verifying their identity.

Funding the wallet

Once the account is set up, the user can fund his/her digital
wallet with money by linking it to a bank account, credit/debit card
or other payment methods. Some may also support direct deposits or transfers.

Security

The digital wallet welfare kit employs various measures
e.g encryption, two-factor authentication and device-specific security
to protect the user's data and funds from unauthorised access.

Storing Payment Information

The digital wallet securely stores the user's payment information
ie. credit/debit card details, bank account numbers.

Online Payment

The user can log into their wallet account
to make payments and transactions.

Budgeting And Expense Tracking

This is done to help them stay within their budget
through managing their spending and tracking their expenses.
