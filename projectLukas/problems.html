Financial Burden

Most of the students usually face strains
in raising tution fees and upkeep money while in school.
They therefore opt to look for jobs for themselves.
This later creates financial stress in them.
They are forced to balance part-time jobs,
cost of living and academic work

Academic Pressure

This is the demanding nature of academic work.
Competition and high expectations lead to:
stress, anxiety and depression

Social Isolation

University life often involves transitioning to a new environment.
This means leaving behind familiar support networks.
Thus one may face new social challanges.

Substance Abuse

Sometimes students turn to:
alcohol, drugs and other mischief
as a coping mechanism for managing stress.
